What is Logistics Course?

The logistics process involves planning, implementing, and controlling how goods and services are transported from their point of origin to their point of consumption. Logistics courses provide students with knowledge and skills related to managing the flow of goods and services.

A logistics course typically covers topics such as transportation management, inventory control, supply chain management, warehousing, and distribution. During this course, students will learn how to optimize the movement of goods through air, sea, and land modes of transportation.

What is Supply-Chain Management Course?

Supply chain management involves coordinating and managing all of the activities involved in producing and delivering goods to customers. A supply chain includes planning, sourcing, manufacturing, and delivering products to customers, as well as managing relationships and partnerships between different entities.

Supply chain management ensures that the right products are delivered to customers at the right time, in the right quantity, with the right quality, and at the right price. In order to achieve this, supply chain managers must streamline processes, reduce costs, and minimize waste throughout the entire supply chain.
